# Smooth Moves - User Manual

## Overview
Smooth Moves is your all-in-one companion for managing your move. It helps you track boxes, manage budgets, schedule tasks, and even includes an AI assistant, MARVIN, to help you stay organized.

## Features

### 1. Dashboard
- **Overview:** View the current status of your move, including box counts and recent activity.
- **Quick Actions:** Access common tasks like scanning a box or adding an expense.

### 2. Box Management (`/app/boxes`, `/app/scan`)
- **Scan & Track:** Use your camera to scan QR codes on boxes to instantly view or update their contents.
- **Box Details:**
  - **Status:** Mark boxes as 'Packing', 'In Transit', 'Unpacked', etc.
  - **Contents:** List items inside the box.
  - **Photos:** Take photos of open boxes to remember what's inside.
- **Truck Load:** Organize boxes for loading onto the moving truck.

### 3. Owners & Spaces (`/app/owners`, `/app/spaces`)
- **Owners:** Assign boxes to family members or movers (color-coded).
- **Spaces:** Define rooms (e.g., "Living Room", "Master Bedroom") to know exactly where each box goes.

### 4. Budget Tracking (`/app/budget`)
- **Expenses:** Log moving-related expenses.
- **Categories:** Group expenses (e.g., "Movers", "Supplies", "Travel").
- **Receipts:** Upload receipts (OCR integration available for automatic scanning).
- **Charts:** Visual breakdown of your spending vs. budget.

### 5. Calendar (`/app/calendar`)
- **Schedule:** Track important dates like packing days, utility disconnects, and the big move day.
- **Views:** Switch between month, week, and day views.

### 6. Planner (`/app/planner`)
- **Tasks:** Manage to-do lists for different phases of the move.
- **Timeframes:** Organize tasks by "Months Before", "Weeks Before", etc.
- **Drag & Drop:** Easily reorder tasks.

### 7. MARVIN AI Assistant (`/app/marvin`)
- **Chat:** Ask MARVIN questions about your move (e.g., "Where did I pack the blender?", "What's my remaining budget?").
- **Voice Commands:** (Optional) Wake MARVIN with a voice command to ask for help hands-free.

## Getting Started
1. **Log In:** Create an account or sign in.
2. **Set Up:** Define your move details, owners, and spaces.
3. **Start Packing:** Print QR codes, stick them on boxes, and start scanning!
