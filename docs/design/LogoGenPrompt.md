Create a modern, scalable logo for the mobile/web app “Smooth Moves” (moving assistant).

  Style: Minimal, flat, and clean with precise geometry. Use a monoline icon style that reads crisply at 24px and scales up elegantly. Favor rounded corners and consistent stroke weight (approx. 2–3px at 24px grid). Avoid gradients in the primary mark (solid fills only); gradients may be used in optional marketing lockups. Design must feel calm, trustworthy, and modern.

  Colors: Use the Smooth Moves brand palette. Primary: #1e3a5f (deep blue) as the dominant brand tone. Secondary: #708090 (slate gray) for neutrals. Accent: #ff7e00 (bright orange) for highlights; optional soft accent #e1a95f. Provide light and dark mode variants and a single-color version (solid white and solid #1e3a5f). Avoid pure black; ensure accessible contrast when the mark appears over backgrounds.

  Imagery: Provide three distinct, modern logo concepts. Each must be unique, avoid resemblance to existing brands, use flat/monoline construction, and fit cleanly within a squircle for app icon usage.
  
  1) S-Path Corner Box (Monoline)
     A flowing “S” line merging into the corner of a simple open box to signal smooth movement and careful packing.
     - Draw a continuous S-shaped path that transitions into the front top-right corner of a minimal open box (two perpendicular lines suggesting lid and side).
     - Keep the S path and box edges the same stroke weight; use rounded stroke caps and 4–6px corner radii (scaled from a 24px grid).
     - Terminate the path with a subtle arrow-like taper or rounded end—no separate arrowhead shape.
     - Compose within a squircle safe area with ~8% padding; ensure clarity at 24px by simplifying intersections to no more than two overlaps.
     - Primary in #1e3a5f; allow a small accent micro-detail in #ff7e00 only for marketing lockups (not required for the core mark).
  
  2) Kinetic Squircle Crate (Negative Space)
     A friendly squircle with a lid cut from negative space and subtle glide cues, reading as a crate in motion without speed clichés.
     - Use a solid squircle base. Carve a simple box “lid” using negative space offset at a 15–20° angle to suggest motion.
     - Add two small, evenly spaced negative-space chevrons along one side to imply glide, not speed; chevrons must not form common brand motifs.
     - Keep all corners consistently rounded; no internal gradients or shadows. Maintain clear space equal to 0.5× icon width.
     - Provide light/dark variants: base in #1e3a5f with negative space on light backgrounds, and inverted (white base with #1e3a5f negative space) on dark.
  
  3) Modular Route Tiles (Geometric)
     Four minimal corner tiles implying a box, with a subtle S-shaped route formed by the negative space through the center.
     - Arrange four L-shaped tiles to imply a box from corners; rotate one tile to subtly create an S-like route through the center via negative space.
     - Use equal stroke weights and 90° geometry with 3–4px internal radii; avoid circles, stars, or pin shapes to prevent map-app resemblance.
     - Optionally color only one tile with #ff7e00 for emphasis in extended lockups; keep the core icon single-color (#1e3a5f).
     - Verify legibility at 24px by merging tiny gaps; avoid any motif that reads as an existing delivery/logistics brand.

  Typography: Use Inter to match app UI. If a custom wordmark is needed, set the logotype in Inter (or a compatible geometric sans like Manrope or Plus Jakarta Sans). Weight 600–700 for the wordmark; tracking -1% to 0% for compactness. Provide horizontal and stacked lockups, aligning letterforms with the icon’s rounded geometry. Avoid script/serif styles.

  Output: Deliver production-ready assets: SVG (icon-only, wordmark, and combined lockups); PNG exports at 1024, 512, 256, 128, 64 on transparent; app icon at 1024×1024 within a squircle-safe area with ~8% padding (no text); favicon set (SVG, 32×32, 16×16). Include light/dark variants, single-color version, color specs, clear space (≥ 0.5× icon width), and minimum size guidance (icon ≥ 24px).
