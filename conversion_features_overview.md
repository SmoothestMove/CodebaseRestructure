# Smooth Moves Mobile App: Features & Capabilities

This document provides a comprehensive overview of the Smooth Moves mobile application's features, functionality, and user experience. It's designed to explain what the app does and how it helps users manage their moving process from start to finish.

## Overview

Smooth Moves is a comprehensive mobile moving assistant that transforms the chaotic process of relocating into an organized, manageable experience. The app combines inventory management, budget tracking, task planning, and intelligent assistance to ensure nothing gets lost, forgotten, or overlooked during your move.

## Core Features

### 1. Authentication & Move Management

**Getting Started**

- Create your account or sign in using email/password or Google authentication
- Start a new move operation or join an existing one using a unique Move ID
- Share your move with family members, roommates, or professional movers for collaborative planning

**Purpose**: Ensures your moving data is secure, synchronized across devices, and accessible to everyone involved in the move.

### 2. Dashboard - Your Move Command Center

**Overview at a Glance**
The Dashboard provides a real-time snapshot of your entire moving operation through an intuitive visual layout:

- **Quick Actions**: Instantly access the most common tasks - scan a box, pack a new item, or browse your inventory
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual indicators show how much you've packed, what's left to do, and your budget status
- **Status Widgets**: See important metrics like total boxes packed, items inventoried, and days until moving day
- **Smart Navigation**: Jump directly to any feature in the app from the central hub

**Purpose**: Gives you instant awareness of your move's status and provides fast access to the tools you need most.

### 3. Scan - The Heart of Inventory Management

**Intelligent QR Code Scanning**
The Scan feature uses your phone's camera to instantly identify and track boxes:

- **Quick Identification**: Point your camera at a QR code label to instantly pull up box details
- **New Box Registration**: Scanning an unrecognized code prompts you to name the box, assign an owner, and describe contents
- **Truck Placement**: After scanning, quickly assign the box to a specific location in the moving truck
- **Status Updates**: One-tap buttons to mark boxes as "Delivered" or "Staged" during moving day
- **Photo Capture**: Take pictures of boxes for visual reference

**Purpose**: Eliminates the confusion of "which box is this?" and creates a searchable, scannable inventory system that works in seconds.

### 4. Inventory Management

**Complete Box Directory**
Browse and manage every box in your move with powerful organization tools:

- **Smart Search**: Find any box instantly by searching for contents, room, owner, or box name
- **Advanced Filtering**: View only boxes with specific statuses (Packed, In Transit, Delivered, etc.)
- **Flexible Sorting**: Organize by date packed, alphabetically, or by weight
- **Detailed Views**: Tap any box to see its complete history, contents, location, and photos

**Box Details**
Each box has a comprehensive profile including:

- Owner assignment (whose belongings are inside)
- Current location (which room or truck zone)
- Complete itemized list of contents
- Photos for visual identification
- Status tracking (packed, loaded, delivered)
- Weight and size information
- Packing date and timeline

**Purpose**: Provides complete visibility into your inventory, making it impossible to lose track of your belongings.

### 5. Pack Box - Guided Packing Workflow

**Step-by-Step Packing Assistant**
A dedicated interface that walks you through packing a new box:

- **Box Identification**: Scan or create a new QR label
- **Owner Assignment**: Specify whose items are being packed
- **Contents Documentation**: List items as you pack them
- **Photo Documentation**: Take pictures of the box and its contents
- **Location Tagging**: Note where the box came from and where it should go

**Purpose**: Ensures every box is properly documented as you pack, preventing the "mystery box" problem at your new home.

### 6. Manage Owners

**Multi-Person Move Organization**
Perfect for roommates, families, or shared moves:

- **Owner Profiles**: Create profiles for each person involved in the move
- **Personal Inventory**: Track which boxes belong to whom
- **Label Generation**: Print QR code labels specific to each owner
- **Statistics**: See how many boxes each person has packed

**Purpose**: Keeps everyone's belongings organized and identifiable, especially important for shared moves or when helping others relocate.

### 7. Manage Spaces

**Room & Location Tracking**
Define and manage the spaces in both your old and new locations:

- **Predefined Rooms**: Quick-select common spaces (Kitchen, Bedroom, Living Room, etc.)
- **Custom Spaces**: Create unique locations (Garage, Storage Unit, Attic, etc.)
- **Space-Specific Labels**: Generate QR codes for room-specific inventory
- **Box Counts**: See how many boxes are assigned to each space

**Purpose**: Helps you organize by room and ensures boxes end up in the right place at your new home.

### 8. Truck Load - Visual Loading Guide

**Interactive Truck Organization**
A visual representation of your moving truck that helps optimize loading:

- **Zone-Based Layout**: The truck is divided into sections (front, middle, back, left, right)
- **Box Assignment**: Assign boxes to specific truck zones for strategic loading
- **Zone Contents**: Tap any zone to see which boxes are assigned there
- **Load Progress**: Visual indicator of how full the truck is

**Purpose**: Helps you load the truck strategically - heavy items first, frequently needed items accessible, fragile items protected.

### 9. Budget - Financial Management

**Complete Expense Tracking**
Monitor every dollar spent on your move:

- **Expense Entry**: Log expenses with descriptions, amounts, and categories
- **Receipt Scanning**: Take photos of receipts for documentation
- **Smart OCR**: Automatically extract expense details from receipt photos
- **Category Management**: Organize expenses into custom categories (Moving Company, Supplies, Deposits, etc.)
- **Budget Setup**: Define your total moving budget and allocate to categories
- **Visual Tracking**: Charts and graphs show spending vs. budget
- **Real-Time Totals**: Always know how much you've spent and how much remains

**Purpose**: Prevents budget overruns and provides complete financial transparency throughout the moving process.

### 10. Calendar - Timeline Management

**Move Schedule Organization**
Track important dates and deadlines:

- **Multiple Views**: Switch between month, week, day, and agenda views
- **Event Creation**: Schedule move-related tasks and milestones
- **Event Details**: Add descriptions, times, and notes to calendar events
- **Move Day Countdown**: Always know how many days until the big day
- **Event Management**: Edit or delete events as plans change

**Purpose**: Keeps you on schedule with lease dates, utility transfers, moving company bookings, and other time-sensitive tasks.

### 11. Planner - Task Management Board

**Comprehensive Task Organization**
A powerful planning board for managing every task in your move:

- **Timeline Frames**: Organize tasks by timeframes (Before Move, Move Day, Week 1, Week 2, etc.)
- **Task Cards**: Create detailed tasks with descriptions, owners, and deadlines
- **Subtasks**: Break large tasks into manageable steps
- **Task Assignment**: Assign tasks to specific people
- **Attachments**: Add notes, photos, or documents to tasks
- **Drag-and-Drop**: Reorganize tasks between timeframes as plans evolve
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual indicators show task completion status

**Purpose**: Ensures nothing falls through the cracks by providing a complete task management system tailored to the moving process.

### 12. Settings - App Configuration

**Move & App Management**
Control your move settings and app preferences:

- **Move ID Display**: View and share your unique move code
- **Data Export**: Generate spreadsheets or JSON files of your entire inventory
- **Move Date**: Set or update your target moving date
- **Reset Options**: Clear data or start a new move
- **App Preferences**: Configure notifications, display options, and other settings

**Purpose**: Provides control over your data and allows you to customize the app to your preferences.

## MARVIN - Your Integrated AI Moving Assistant

**Intelligent Help Throughout the App**

MARVIN is not a separate feature or chatbot - it's an intelligent assistant woven throughout the entire Smooth Moves experience. MARVIN provides contextual help, suggestions, and automation wherever you need it:

**Smart Suggestions**

- Recommends which boxes to pack next based on your timeline
- Suggests optimal truck loading strategies based on your inventory
- Identifies potential budget issues before they become problems
- Proposes task priorities based on your move date

**Contextual Assistance**

- Answers questions about app features right where you're working
- Provides packing tips when you're documenting box contents
- Offers budget advice when entering expenses
- Suggests task breakdowns when creating complex to-dos

**Intelligent Automation**

- Auto-categorizes expenses based on descriptions
- Suggests box contents based on the assigned room
- Recommends task timeframes based on your move date
- Identifies duplicate entries or potential errors

**Natural Interaction**

- Ask questions in plain language anywhere in the app
- Get instant answers without leaving your current screen
- Receive proactive notifications about important tasks or issues
- Learn from your preferences and adapt suggestions over time

**Purpose**: MARVIN makes the app smarter and more helpful by providing AI-powered assistance that's always available, always contextual, and always learning from how you work.

## Executive Summary (4000 Characters)

Smooth Moves is a comprehensive mobile application that eliminates relocation chaos through QR-code-based inventory tracking, collaborative task management, budget monitoring, and integrated AI assistance. The app ensures nothing gets lost, forgotten, or overlooked during moves of any size.

**Core Functionality:** The app centers on a QR code scanning system creating a searchable digital inventory of every box. Users print QR labels, scan as they pack, and instantly document contents, ownership, location, and photos. This eliminates "mystery boxes" and makes finding items as simple as searching your phone. Scanning works offline for moving day reliability.

**Inventory Management:** Each box receives a detailed profile: owner assignment (critical for roommate/family moves), itemized contents, identification photos, location tracking (room or truck zone), status updates (packed/loaded/delivered), and packing timeline. Advanced search and filtering locate any box instantly by contents, owner, room, or status. The system handles hundreds of boxes with optimized mobile performance.

**Collaborative Features:** Multiple users access the same move via shared Move ID, perfect for families, roommates, or professional movers. Manage Owners creates separate inventories while maintaining unified views. Task assignment ensures everyone knows responsibilities, and shared budget tracking provides financial transparency.

**Budget Control:** Complete expense tracking with receipt photo capture and OCR technology automatically extracting amounts, dates, and merchants. Users set budgets, create custom categories (moving company, supplies, deposits, utilities), and receive visual progress indicators. Real-time totals prevent overspending; documentation supports reimbursements and tax deductions.

**Task & Timeline Management:** The Planner provides a visual task board organized by timeframes (Before Move, Move Week, Move Day, Week 1 After). Tasks support subtasks, assignments, attachments, notes, and deadlines. Calendar tracks important dates like lease endings, utility transfers, and bookings, with moving day countdown. These tools prevent critical tasks from being forgotten.

**Truck Loading Optimization:** Interactive truck visualization divides vehicles into zones (front, middle, back, left, right). Users assign boxes to zones during scanning, creating strategic loading plans. Tap zones to view contents, ensuring heavy items load first, frequently-needed items stay accessible, and fragile boxes are protected. Visual indicators show capacity utilization.

**MARVIN AI Integration:** MARVIN offers smart packing suggestions based on timelines, recommends truck loading strategies, identifies budget issues, proposes task priorities, auto-categorizes expenses, suggests box contents based on rooms, and answers natural language questions without leaving screens. The AI learns preferences and adapts suggestions over time.

**Space Management:** Define rooms and storage locations using predefined templates (Kitchen, Bedrooms, Garage) or custom spaces (Storage Unit, Attic). Generate room-specific QR labels and track box counts per location.

**Data Control:** Complete ownership with data export to spreadsheets or JSON, move sharing via unique codes, and reset options. Dashboard provides real-time progress visualization, quick-action buttons, and status widgets showing boxes, items, budget, and days remaining.

**Use Cases:** Solo movers gain complete organization. Families coordinate multi-person moves with clear ownership. Roommates maintain separate inventories while sharing logistics. Professional movers document belongings for insurance and delivery verification. The app scales from studios to large homes, supporting any move size while maintaining responsive performance throughout the relocation journey.
