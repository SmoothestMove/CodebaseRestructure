### Target Users & Use Cases

*   Who will be the primary users? DIY Movers (no professional movers or hired companies)
*   Are you focusing on local moves only, long-distance, or both? Both

### Core Problems to Solve

*   What are the biggest pain points your users face today? stress and emotional overwhelm, Uncertain costs, complex logistics, Exhausting packing and decluttering, Risk of damage or loss of belongings, Post-move settling-in challenges
*   Which of those feels most urgent or underserved by existing apps?complex llogistics, packing and decluittering, stress

### Key Features & Workflows

*   Do you envision an inventory system? Yes, already implemented (individual family member level QR code tracking system )
*   Will users need automated cost estimates (fuel, labor, materials)? helpful, not required
*   Should the app handle bookings and dispatch for moving crews, or just guide the end-user? Just a guide, its intention is to provide a better experience for the user withouot having to pay for a moving company

### Integration & Data Sources

*   Will you pull in third-party services (e.g. truck rental APIs, storage facility locators, moving truck availability)? AI chat bot for search, planning and recommendations.
*   Do you need to integrate with calendars, maps, or payment gateways? Calendars and maps

### Collaboration & User Roles

*   Will multiple users collaborate (e.g. family members, roommates, move coordinators)? Yes there is a moveID code based system for collaboration
*   Do you need role-based permissions (admin, viewer, packer, driver)? Yes, that is a planned feature

### Timeline & Milestones

*   Do you want a step-by-step “move timeline” (packing deadlines, utility cut-off dates, lease end reminders)? yes, oone that can be intelligently provide custom and personalized recommendations based on the users needs
*   Would push-notifications or in-app reminders be critical? helpful, not critical

### Platform & Tech Constraints

*   Is this a mobile-first experience (iOS/Android), a web app, desktop, or hybrid? web app with a mobile first design
*   Any back-end preferences or existing infrastructure we need to align with? using firebase for the backend

### Success Metrics

*   How will you measure “viable” or “worthwhile”? one that is based on the apps purpose wherein it is statistically helpful by ensuring it helps to relieve pain points of moving. It needs to  address the collaborative aspect, should be easily accessible for users and the AI integration.
