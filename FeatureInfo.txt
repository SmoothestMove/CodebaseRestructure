Smooth Moves — Feature Overview

Your all-in-one workspace for planning, packing, tracking, and executing a residential move. 
Smooth Moves connects QR-labeled boxes, owners/spaces, 
planning, calendar, budgeting with receipt OCR, and an in-app AI assistant—securely backed by 
Firebase and designed for clarity and speed.

----------------------------------------
Moves: The Organizing Hub
----------------------------------------
Every project in Smooth Moves is a “Move.” 
It acts as the secure container for all related data—boxes, owners/spaces, calendar, planner, 
and budget—so you always know where things live and who can see them.

What it does
- Groups everything for a relocation into one place
- Supports participants and roles so you can collaborate
- Enforces access with Firebase Auth and Firestore rules

Why it helps
- Keeps family, roommates, and helpers on the same page
- Reduces scatter across spreadsheets, notes, and photo albums
- Provides a trustworthy, privacy-safe record of your move

----------------------------------------
Box Tracking with QR Codes
----------------------------------------
Label boxes once, gain instant traceability forever. 
Generate QR codes, scan with your phone, and instantly view or update each box’s contents, owner, 
and destination room.

Key capabilities
- QR labeling and scanning: Create codes, print or display, and scan to open the box record fast
- Rich box details: Title, description, photos, status, and categories/tags
- Owner/space assignment: Tie each box to a person and destination room
- Search and filters: Find boxes by status, owner, space, or keywords
- Load planning: Use the truck load view to stage and prioritize packing order

Why you’ll love it
- Stop guessing what’s in each box
- Answer “Where did that go?” instantly on move day
- Keep fragile and priority items top-of-mind when loading

----------------------------------------
Owners & Spaces
----------------------------------------
Assign boxes to owners (people) and spaces (rooms) to make packing and unpacking logical, fast, and fair.

What it does
- Owners: Track who a box belongs to (e.g., Sam, Jamie, Kids)
- Spaces: Define source/destination rooms (e.g., Kitchen, Office)
- Quick assignment: Apply owners/spaces in bulk or box-by-box

Benefits
- Simplifies day-of-move routing (“This goes to the new Office”)
- Keeps personal items private and easy to locate later
- Helps balance effort across family members or roommates

----------------------------------------
Planner (Tasks, Timeframes, Configuration)
----------------------------------------
Turn moving chaos into a plan you can actually follow. 
Break work into tasks, group them into timeframes (e.g., “4 weeks out,” “Move week”), and track progress.

Core features
- Tasks: Title, description, status, owner, due date, and links
- Timeframes: Organize tasks by phase of the move
- Config: Customize categories, templates, and defaults

Why it matters
- Reduces last-minute stress with clear, phased steps
- Improves accountability when multiple people are helping
- Keeps important prep (utilities, address changes) from slipping through

----------------------------------------
Calendar
----------------------------------------
Your move has a timeline. The calendar makes it visible. 
See events, deadlines, deliveries, and appointment windows alongside your planning tasks.

Highlights
- Event management: Add, edit, and view move-specific events
- Day/week/month views: Navigate near-term detail and long-range planning
- Context links: Jump from events to related tasks or boxes

Outcomes
- Avoid missed deliveries and service appointments
- Coordinate helpers around real timelines
- Keep the whole move on track at a glance

----------------------------------------
Budgeting with Receipt OCR
----------------------------------------
Track costs without drowning in paperwork. Snap photos of receipts; 
Smooth Moves extracts key details to categorize expenses and keep your budget up to date.

Capabilities
- Categories and totals: Build a budget with real-time rollups
- Receipt OCR: Extracts vendor, date, amount, currency, and line items when available
- Attachments: Store receipt images with each expense
- Filters: View spend by category, timeframe, or payee

Why it’s powerful
- Prevents overspend surprises with clear category totals
- Automates the boring parts of expense entry
- Keeps documentation in one place for reimbursements or taxes

----------------------------------------
MARVIN: Your In‑App AI Assistant
----------------------------------------
Get quick, context-aware help without leaving the app. 
Ask MARVIN to suggest packing checklists, draft timelines, estimate supplies, or find items across 
your boxes.

What MARVIN can do
- Planning suggestions: Generate phased task lists and reminders
- Research assistance: Offer move tips tailored to your situation
- Content drafting: Create messages to landlords, movers, or helpers
- Context lookups: Help locate boxes or flag potential gaps
- Voice support: Optional wake word and speech capabilities for hands-free use

Why it helps
- Saves hours on planning and coordination
- Reduces decision fatigue with sensible defaults and checklists
- Makes you feel in control during a high-stress period

----------------------------------------
Security, Sync, and Reliability (Firebase)
----------------------------------------
Smooth Moves uses Firebase for authentication, data, and files—so your move is private, always available, and consistent across devices.

Under the hood
- Auth: Sign-in with Firebase Authentication; only participants can access a move
- Firestore: Real-time updates to boxes, tasks, calendar, and budgets
- Storage: Attach photos and receipts securely
- Hosting: Fast, global delivery for a snappy app experience

What this means for you
- Data privacy by default: Only invited participants can view or edit
- Multi-device sync: Phone, tablet, laptop—your move is always in step
- Peace of mind: A reliable backend so you can focus on moving

----------------------------------------
Who It’s For
----------------------------------------
- Families and roommates coordinating a complex move
- Frequent movers who need a repeatable system
- Organizers and assistants managing moves for clients
- Anyone who wants less chaos and more clarity on move day

----------------------------------------
Common Scenarios
----------------------------------------
- Pre-move planning: Generate a phased checklist with MARVIN; schedule key dates on the calendar
- Packing week: Label boxes with QR codes; assign owners/spaces; track fragile items
- Move day: Use truck load planning; route boxes to destination rooms; find essentials fast
- Post-move: Search boxes for specific items; reconcile receipts; close remaining tasks

----------------------------------------
Getting Started
----------------------------------------
1) Create a new Move and invite participants
2) Add owners and destination spaces (rooms)
3) Start your Planner with core tasks and timelines
4) Label boxes with QR codes as you pack
5) Track your budget—scan receipts as you go
6) Ask MARVIN for suggestions any time you’re stuck
